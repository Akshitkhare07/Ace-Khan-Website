ACE-KHAN FILMS Presents

Bhopal International Film Festival

A bold new chapter for independent cinema, regional talent and global storytellers.

The Bhopal International Film Festival is a dynamic celebration of independent filmmaking, cultural storytelling, and regional filmmakers. Curated by ACE-KHAN FILMS in partnership with private film organizations, cultural groups and festival collaborators, BIFF highlights work from India and around the world while supporting local talent from Madhya Pradesh.

Organized By

Private film organizations, event partners and independent film societies, with occasional support from MP film bodies.

Festival Vision

Empower filmmakers, connect audiences, and showcase cinema that matters across cultures and communities.

Festival Pillars

Built around the spirit of independent cinema, global stories, regional talent and creative collaboration.

Independent Cinema

Platform for filmmakers who tell bold, original stories outside the mainstream.

Global Storytelling

Showcase films from across the world and present them to a new Indian audience.

Regional Talent

Spotlight on Hindi and Madhya Pradesh creators who are shaping the next wave.

Creative Community

Networking, panels, workshops and red carpet moments for film professionals and fans.

Festival Categories

BIFF highlights the full spectrum of contemporary filmmaking, from feature narratives to experimental work.

Feature Films

Films with compelling stories, strong direction and cinematic ambition.

Short Films

Concise, high-impact storytelling with memorable characters and visuals.

Documentaries

Truth-driven stories that explore real life, culture and social issues.

Student Films

Emerging voices from film schools and the next generation of cinema makers.

Experimental Cinema

Boundary-pushing work that explores new forms, textures and cinematic language.

Music Videos

Visually rich music films that blend sound, performance and style.

Awards & Recognition

BIFF celebrates excellence across categories and gives winners recognition, trophies, certificates and sometimes cash or distribution support.

  • Best Feature Film
  • Best Short Film
  • Best Director
  • Best Actor / Actress
  • Best Documentary
  • Jury Awards

Winning entries are often honored with trophies, certificates and opportunities to gain wider exposure.

Key Activities

BIFF combines screenings, discussions, workshops, networking and celebration to create an immersive festival experience.

Film Screenings

Showcasing curated films across theaters and event venues.

Panel Discussions

Conversations with filmmakers, critics and industry professionals.

Workshops

Sessions on acting, directing, editing and storytelling craft.

Networking

Opportunities for creators, producers and artists to connect.

Red Carpet Night

A glamorous awards evening with talent, media and celebration.

Community Events

Sessions designed to engage Bhopal audiences with film culture.

Be part of BIFF

Whether you are a filmmaker, student, sponsor or audience member, BIFF invites you to share stories and celebrate cinema in Bhopal.
